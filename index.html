FAQ

Why autonomy is critical on Mars?

Average round-trip delay can reach tens of minutes, so local decision-making is required.

What data does AI help analyze?

Imagery, spectra, telemetry, weather, terrain meshes, and simulation outputs.

Where to start building skills?

Python, data analysis, control, computer vision, and embedded systems.
